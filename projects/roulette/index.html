How to Play

🎯 Objective

Predict where the ball will land on the spinning wheel.

🎰 The Wheel

37 pockets numbered 0-36. Numbers are red, black, or green (0).

💰 Placing Bets

1. Select a chip value
2. Click on the table to place bets
3. Click SPIN when ready

🎲 Bet Types

  • Single Number: Bet on one number (35:1)
  • Red/Black: Bet on color (1:1)
  • Odd/Even: Bet on parity (1:1)
  • 1-18/19-36: Bet on range (1:1)
  • Dozens: Bet on 12 numbers (2:1)
  • Columns: Bet on a column (2:1)

⚠️ The Zero

If 0 lands, all outside bets (color, odd/even, etc.) lose. Only bets on 0 win.

How It's Calculated

📊 Win Probability

Calculated as:

P = (Winning Outcomes ÷ 37) × 100%

Example: Single number has 1÷37 = 2.7% chance

💵 Payout Ratio

Shows how much you win per chip bet:

  • 35:1 = Win 35 chips + your bet
  • 2:1 = Win 2 chips + your bet
  • 1:1 = Win 1 chip + your bet

📈 Expected Value (EV)

The mathematical average outcome:

EV = (P×Payout) - ((1-P)×Bet)

Negative EV means house has an edge. In European roulette, EV is always -2.7%
