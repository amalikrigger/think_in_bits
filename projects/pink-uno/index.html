🎴 How to Play UNO

Objective

Be the first player to empty your hand. Shout UNO! when you have one card left.

On Your Turn

  • Play a card that matches the top card by color, number, or symbol.
  • If you can't play, click the draw pile to draw one card.
  • If the drawn card is playable, you may play it immediately.

Special Cards

  • Skip — Next player loses their turn.
  • Reverse — Play direction reverses.
  • Draw 2 — Next player draws 2 cards and is skipped. Can be stacked!
  • Wild — Play on anything; choose any color.
  • Wild Draw 4 — Choose a color; next player draws 4 and is skipped.

Draw 2 Stacking

If hit with a Draw 2, you can play your own Draw 2 to stack the penalty. The next player then faces the full accumulated draw count, or can stack further.

🩷 Pink Cards (Special)

  • Pink Bomb — All other players draw 2 cards.
  • Pink Shuffle — All hands are collected, shuffled, and redistributed randomly.
  • Pink Peek & Steal — Peek at any opponent's hand and steal one card from them.
  • Pink Shield — Protects you from the next action card that targets you (Draw 2, Wild Draw 4, Skip).

Pink cards can be played on any turn, but only activate their ability when played.

Winning

Play your last card to win! UNO is automatically declared when you have one card left.
